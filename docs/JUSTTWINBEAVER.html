JUST TWIN BEAVER

Project Background

This game was developed during 4 days for ComJamOn 2025, a local game jam hosted at Universidad Complutense of Madrid. The theme of the jam was "Branches".

We formed the team Huevos Revueltos. I participated as a programmer, mainly working with Unity and C#.

It was my first time working exclusively with designers, artists, and sound specialists, and the experience couldn’t have been better! We collaborated intensively and efficiently, with ideas flowing freely and mutual support pushing the project forward.

The final result was Just Twin Beaver, a game that emerged from a shared creative vision, developed under tight deadlines but with a strong spirit of teamwork and experimentation.

What is Just Twin Beaver?

Just Twin Beaver is a chaotic competitive game where twin beavers, Tady and Dom, battle to destroy each other's dam first—while keeping their own intact. The twist? You have to share the controls! Each player uses a stick, leading to either teamwork or total disaster. And of course, conflicts are settled by flinging cans using sticks held in your mouths.

Controls

Keyboard or Gamepad

  • Player 1: WASD or Left Stick
  • Player 2: Arrow Keys or Right Stick
  • Gamepad: Press Start / Option to begin

Why should you play this?

  • Angry beavers in a legendary sibling showdown.
  • High-speed can fights that defy both physics and logic.
  • We love beavers. You love beavers.
  • Also, how many games let you throw cans with a stick in your mouth? Exactly.
