AMON-RA

This project consists of the creation of a challenging platformer game using Unity, where the player must navigate through three levels aiming to complete them with the fewest deaths possible. The game mechanics are simple, but the difficulty lies in the precision and timing required to overcome obstacles. The Tiled tool from Unity was used to design the levels, allowing for easy management of tile-based environments. Each level presents increasingly difficult challenges, pushing the player to master the mechanics and improve their skills. The game design focuses on offering a tough but fair experience, rewarding patience and perseverance as the player attempts to complete the levels with minimal mistakes.
