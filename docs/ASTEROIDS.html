ASTEROIDS

In this project, we used SDL to recreate the classic arcade game Asteroids. The player's objective is to destroy all asteroids by shooting at them. If you get hit three times, you lose the game. There are two types of asteroids in the game: regular ones, which move in a fixed direction, and golden ones, which chase the player.

The game logic was structured using a system-based architecture, allowing us to organize functions into specific modules. One of these systems handles the online mode, enabling players to engage in PvP matches with another player if both devices are on the same network and share their IP address. This architecture not only simplified the development process but also made it easier to integrate extra features, such as online gameplay, creating a more dynamic and engaging experience for players.
